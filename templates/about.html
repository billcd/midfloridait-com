About Mid Florida IT

Who We Are

Mid Florida IT is a locally owned technology services company serving businesses across Central Florida. We specialize in delivering dependable, straightforward IT solutions that let you focus on running your business instead of troubleshooting technology.

Our Approach

We believe IT support should be proactive, not reactive. Our team monitors your systems around the clock so problems are resolved before they impact your operations. When you call us, you talk to a real person who knows your environment — not a ticket queue.

Why Businesses Choose Us

  • Vendor-neutral recommendations — we suggest what works, not what pays us the most
  • Experience across industries including healthcare, legal, finance, and manufacturing
